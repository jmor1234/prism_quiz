## Founder Origin Story

Dalton Graham studied chemical and biomolecular engineering at Tulane University. Developed significant gastrointestinal problems and consulted top doctors who offered no solutions. Through self-directed scientific research and experimentation, he resolved his own health issues. This personal experience — combined with scientific literacy from his engineering background — is the foundation of Prism.

## Program Structure (detailed timeline)

### Pre-Program
- Take-home assessment: temperature, heart rate, diet tracking
- 60+ research-backed symptom/history questions
- Prior lab analysis

### Phase 1: Deep Assessment (Day 1 – Weeks 2-3)
- Day 1: Team analysis of submitted data
- Week 1: Primary advisor assignment (matched to client's symptoms)
- Week 2: Deep-dive video consultation (map symptoms, history, patterns)
- Weeks 2-3: Comprehensive report with systemic review, interpretation, backed by 1000+ peer-reviewed studies
- Sample report PDF available for prospects

### Phase 2: Implementation & Long-Term Strategy (Months 1-4+)
- Month 1: Launch protocol, precision discounted labs, personalized plan execution
- Month 2: Weekly adjustments via messaging, symptom-based tweaks
- Month 3: Optional follow-up labs, diet/supplement/habit refinement
- Month 4+: Long-term roadmap for sustainable, compounding progress
