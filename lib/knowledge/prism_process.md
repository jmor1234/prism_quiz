# Our Process

Discover how our comprehensive, team-based approach delivers personalized health solutions tailored just for you.

## How It Works

*A step-by-step journey to optimal health with our expert team.*

### 1. First Call

Schedule a complimentary free call. Before your call, you'll be able to give us a brief background on your goals and other accompanying information.

On the call, we'll dive deep into your history, expectations coming in, and how we'll be able to support you through your health journey. We will walk you through all of our extensive package and pricing options to best find the plan that suits your needs.

### 2. Join Portal

Join our seamless and secure client portal. Our portal is your one-stop shop for everything you need.

Upload all of your lab work, have your consultations, view your reports, and message the team through SimplePractice. Everything is streamlined and in one location so you can focus on what really matters.

### 3. Intake Forms

We get a comprehensive overview of your history, symptoms, current regimen, lab work, and more to ensure the highest quality of care.

This includes an extensive health survey and a take-home assessment to track certain measurements in your everyday life. No guesswork, no generic protocols. Everything is tailored to suit your needs.

### 4. Advisor Assignment

Based on your information, we will pair you with an advisor who has the experience and knowledge to match your goals.

Your advisor will be your principal point of contact along your journey with us, working one-on-one with them as your personal health expert.

### 5. Consultation

Part of working with your advisor involves in-depth, one-on-one video consultations. Here, you'll go even deeper into your history and goals — discussing and reasoning through what could be going on, and forming a preliminary plan of action.

### 6. Team Review

Your advisor diligently notes the key points made during your consultation. They then present the notes to the rest of the team — including two other advisors with unique areas of expertise. Together, the team formulates a final plan for you and your advisor to work on together.

### 7. Report

That plan is explicitly documented in detail with our client care reports. Your report will include analysis on any existing lab work, as well as your survey and take-home assessments.

Everything is explained in terms of its relevance to your specific case, so you can better understand your signs, symptoms, and lab work. The report also includes the team's full slate of recommendations — from diet and lifestyle to supplements.

No further research necessary: each recommendation includes detail on the *how* as well as the *why*, so you don't have to do any homework.

### 8. Messaging

After you receive your report, the support doesn't end. You will have around-the-clock messaging access to your primary advisor for any questions, updates, suggestions, or just to talk.

During this time, your advisor may reconvene with the rest of the team to further collaborate for a better perspective.

### 9. Continued Support

Depending on the plan you chose, you'll have multiple opportunities for further consultations, reports, and time to message your advisor. The team is here to support you for as long as you need to achieve your goals.
